Wasteland, Baby!

March 1, 2019

This was Hozier's second studio album and involves songs from his EP "Nina Cried Power" and one of his singles. Hozier took a break after releasing his first album and during this break her became a news junkie. He wrote most of the songs in this album after seeing the humanitarian crisises and political turmoil of the time.
